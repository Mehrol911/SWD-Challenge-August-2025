Purpose & Usage

This guide ensures consistent, accessible, and memorable visualizations for Al-Misbah's teaching materials and dashboards. Use for: workshop slides, monthly reports, social media infographics, and Qlik dashboards.

✅ Do:

Use brand orange for emphasis, direct labels on charts, clear legible fonts.

❌ Don't:

Use rainbow palettes, 3D charts, or small text under 10 px.

Logo & Clearspace

المصباح

Minimum clearspace = height of the logo on all sides.

Do not rotate, recolor outside brand palette, or distort. Use the monochrome variant only on single-color backgrounds.

Typography & Text Rules

Font Selection

Arabic font: Noto Naskh Arabic / Noto Sans Arabic

Latin/Russian: Poppins or Inter

Sizes (slides/dashboards)

Title: 30-36 px | Subtitle: 18-22 px | Body: 12-14 px | Axis: 10-12 px

RTL Notes

Mirror layouts; ensure Arabic labels are right-aligned and have at least 1.2 line-height.

Layout, Exports & Accessibility

📐 Slide Specifications

Slide size: 16:9 (1920×1080)

Qlik sheet thumbnails: 1536×1024

♿ Accessibility

Ensure text contrast (AA), font sizes >= 12 pt for slide body, provide alt text for exported images, and check colorblind readability.

📤 Export Formats

PDF for print; PPTX for editing; PNG/SVG for web assets.
